🔧 Key Architectural Improvements

1. Dilated Convolutions

Expanded receptive fields for better context understanding of crop symptoms and disease patterns.

2. Enhanced FPN

Multi-scale feature pyramid with improved fusion for detecting diseases at different scales.

3. Attention Mechanisms

Channel and spatial attention modules to focus on disease-relevant features.

4. Decoupled Heads

Separate branches for classification and localization for better specialization.
